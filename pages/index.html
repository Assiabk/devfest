What is DevFest?

DevFest is an annual event organized by GDG groups, bringing developers together for workshops 💻, hackathons 🏆, and networking 🤝 to explore the latest technologies. GDG Ghardaia, a local chapter in Algeria, hosts these events to foster skill development and community connections among tech enthusiasts in the region.

It’s all about learning and growing together, empowering the community to build amazing things with technology.
